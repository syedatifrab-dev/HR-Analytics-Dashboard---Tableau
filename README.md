## The dashboard monitors five critical high-level metrics:

-Total Employees: Overall workforce size.

-Attrition Count: Number of employees who have left the organization.

-Attrition Rate: Percentage of turnover.

-Active Employees: Current total headcount.

-Average Age: The mean age of the workforce.
